## Key Risk Disclosure

BABA carries elevated structural risks not present in US equities:
- **VIE structure:** US holders own economic interests in a Cayman holding company, not direct China equity — enforceability of shareholder rights depends on Chinese contract law.

- **China regulatory risk:** Chinese authorities have demonstrated willingness to act against domestic tech platforms; repeat of 2021 action cannot be excluded.

These risks are priced in at 1.68x P/B, but they are real and require active monitoring.
