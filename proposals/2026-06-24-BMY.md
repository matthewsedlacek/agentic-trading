## Rationale for Escalation

satisfied. However, the trade is escalated for two reasons:

1. **Buying power insufficient:** Account buying power is $13.45, below the

2. **Protective orders cannot be placed on fractional shares:** Robinhood does
   not support stop_market, stop_limit, or limit sell orders on fractional

   required by policy cannot be set up. If the operator accepts this risk
   (manual monitoring via hourly runs), a smaller fractional position within
   buying power (~$13, ~0.23 shares) could be entered interactively.

$25 per-trade cap and current buying power. A whole-share entry with proper
protective orders is not feasible at this account size.
