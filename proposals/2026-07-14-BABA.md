## Structural Risk Disclosure
BABA trades as an American Depositary Share (ADS) backed by a **Variable Interest Entity (VIE)** structure: investors own shares in a Cayman Islands holding company that holds contractual rights to Alibaba's China operating entities — NOT direct equity ownership in those entities. Chinese law prohibits direct foreign ownership of core internet assets; the VIE structure is an unenforceable-by-US-courts contractual workaround. The operator must independently weigh this structural risk before executing. The P/B of 1.70 may already discount some VIE risk, but a forced VIE unwind would likely impair ADS value materially.
