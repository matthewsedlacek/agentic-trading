## ⚠️ ADR / VIE Risk Disclosure
BABA trades as an American Depositary Receipt (ADR) representing shares in a Cayman Islands holding company that has contractual arrangements (VIE structure) with its China operating entities. ADR holders do **not** have direct ownership of the operating businesses. This structure is subject to:
- Chinese regulatory changes that could unwind or restrict VIE arrangements
- US-China geopolitical escalation that could lead to mandatory delisting from US exchanges
- Capital repatriation restrictions

These tail risks are not captured in a quantitative P/E or P/B screen. The operator must weigh these explicitly before approving.
